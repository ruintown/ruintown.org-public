Never dead; temporarily gone.

Ruintown was originally a multi-use live-in venue, skate, and workshop space.

It was created by Glenn Gentzke, Logan, and Dan DeFrancisco in beautiful Baltimore, MD.

Ongoing it is a placeholder for Glenn's dreams and may come to fruition some day. Here's hoping!
